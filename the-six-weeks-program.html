A 6-Week Preventive Wellbeing Programme

Sustaining Self Under Pressure

You are not alone in what you’re carrying. Many people experience challenges that others may not see or fully understand. Even when you appear strong — showing up, smiling, and pushing through — sustained stress can quietly take a toll on your health, energy, and sense of self. I have been there, and I understand.

I created this programme for educators and professionals who are functioning, capable, and committed — yet depleted. For those who want practical support to work through internal strain, shift unhelpful patterns, and move forward with greater clarity and sustainability.

Sustaining Self Under Pressure is a structured six-week programme grounded in preventive wellbeing principles. It is educational, not therapeutic, and focuses on early intervention, stress literacy, and self-leadership — supporting you before stress escalates into burnout.

As a certified professional, I guide you through practical learning, reflection, and application to help you recognise that you are enough and worthy of care. Together, we focus on building awareness, strengthening regulation, and creating realistic habits that support your health, capacity, and performance over time. This is about breathing more freely, reconnecting with yourself, and learning how to sustain the life and work you care about.

What the Programme Includes

  • Structured weekly sessions (1:1 or small group)
  • Guided learning and practical exercises for stress awareness and regulation
  • Weekly reflection activities to strengthen mind–body connection
  • Tools and resources to support sustainable habits beyond the programme
  • Optional 1:1 sessions following completion
    • Participants who choose 1:1 support may also be guided through additional reflective resources tailored to their individual needs.

Primary Goals for Emotional Healing

Emotional healing is a reflective and integrative process supporting awareness, growth, and healthier ways of relating to yourself and others.

Coping with Past Experiences

Developing healthier ways to understand, process, and move forward from difficult or painful experiences.

Shifting Unhelpful Patterns

Increasing awareness of habits and behaviours that no longer serve you, and learning practical ways to respond differently.

Building Healthier Relationships

Strengthening communication, boundaries, and connection in personal and professional relationships.

Personal Growth and Self-Discovery

Exploring values, purpose, and self-leadership to support meaningful and sustainable change.

Six-Week Programme Structure

Week 1: Understanding Stress and the Body

Stress literacy: how chronic stress impacts the nervous system, energy, and decision-making

Week 2: Early Warning Signs and Burnout Prevention

Recognising stress signals, triggers, and early warning signs before burnout occurs

Week 3: Regulation and Recovery

Practical tools to support nervous system regulation, rest, and sustainable energy

Week 4: Boundaries, Capacity, and Self-Leadership

Understanding limits, reducing depletion, and creating healthier patterns of engagement

Week 5: Meaning, Motivation, and Values

Reconnecting with purpose as a protective factor against burnout

Week 6: Integration and Sustainability

Creating a personalised preventive wellbeing plan to support ongoing health and resilience
