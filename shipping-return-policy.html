Shipping & Refund Policy

Thank you for choosing to shop with Marsha Kerr Talley. We are committed to providing our valued customers with the best shopping experience and have implemented a transparent and reliable policy to support your satisfaction.

Shipping Policy

Shipping Charges

We provide free shipping on all orders over £50. For orders under £50, a flat rate of £5.99 applies on eligible items.

Delivery Time

We understand that timely delivery is important and strive to ensure your order is delivered as quickly as possible. We estimate 2–3 business days for UK orders.

Order Processing

We generally process orders within 1–2 business days of receiving them. Once your order is shipped, you will receive an email notification with tracking information.

Delivery Partners

We have partnered with reliable courier services, such as DPD, Royal Mail, and Hermes, to ensure your order is delivered safely and securely.

Delivery Address

Customers must provide accurate and complete delivery addresses to ensure timely deliveries. Please note that we are not responsible for delays or failed deliveries due to incorrect or incomplete delivery addresses.

International Shipping

We currently do not offer international shipping. We only ship to addresses in the UK.

If you have any questions or concerns about our shipping policy, please email us at info@marshakerrtalley.com.

Refund Policy

We offer a 30-day refund policy, after which no returns or exchanges are allowed.

Eligibility for Refunds

  • Your item must be unused and in the same condition you received it.
  • The item must be in the original packaging.
  • To complete your return, we require a receipt or proof of purchase.
  • Only regular priced items may be refunded; we do not refund sale items.
  • Exchanges are not available.
  • Partial refunds are not available.

Refund Approval & Processing

We'll email you to confirm once we receive and inspect your return. If approved, we will process your refund within a few days.

Late or Missing Refunds

If you have not received a refund, contact your bank or credit card company. It may take a few days for the refund to be posted. If you have done all of this and you still have not received your refund yet, please get in touch with us at info@marshakerrtalley.com.

Shipping (Returns)

  • Please note that you are responsible for paying the shipping costs for returning your item.
  • Shipping costs are non-refundable. If you receive a refund, the cost of return shipping will be deducted from your refund.
  • Please note we cannot guarantee that we will receive your returned item.
For refund support, email: info@marshakerrtalley.com

Need help? Email us anytime at info@marshakerrtalley.com.
