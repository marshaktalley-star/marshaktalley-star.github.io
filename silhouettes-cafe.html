Founder Roots

Rooted in Jamaica. Guided by Healing.

Before Silhouette’s Café became a wellness vision, healing was already a part of my life.

In Jamaica, I didn’t grow up relying on pharmaceutical medicine. Wellness came from nature, and there was always a bush for that.

When I was six years old, I fell and split my head on a rock. Instead of rushing to a hospital, my family treated me with banana sucker and other bush medicine to stop the bleeding and support the healing naturally.

That experience stayed with me. It shaped the way I viewed plants, wellness, and the body’s connection to nature.

Silhouette’s Café is more than a café concept. It is a return to the healing traditions that raised me.

It is only fitting that this vision returns to the island that first gave me life.
