Silhouette’s Café™

Nourish. Heal. Thrive.

A wellness café concept rooted in herbal coffee, teas, essential oils, natural skincare, healthy drinks, movement, plants, water, sunlight, community, and sustainable living.

Originally founded in Texas and now being reimagined for Jamaica, Silhouette’s Café is more than a café. It is a lifestyle, a healing space, and a place to recharge.

See the Vision Read Reviews

The Story

From a small wellness booth to a full tropical café vision.

Silhouette’s Café began as Marsha Kerr Talley’s wellness brand, offering essential oils, herbal coffee, teas, natural skincare, diffusers, apparel, and education around alternative wellness.

The next chapter is a small but intentional wellness café in Jamaica, a place where guests can enjoy fresh smoothies, herbal teas, healthy coffee, simple tropical fruit blends, natural products, and empowering movement experiences such as Silhouette Strike Sessions.

What Silhouette’s Café Will Offer

A grounded, relatable, eco-conscious wellness experience that feels warm, tropical, energizing, and real.

Café & Juice Bar

Healthy coffee, herbal teas, smoothies, wellness shots, fruit cups, pastries, and healthier boba-inspired drinks.

Herbal Wellness

Essential oils, herbs, spices, diffusers, natural skincare, and education around everyday wellness rituals.

Silhouette Strike Sessions

High-energy combat fitness experiences for people who heal through movement, strength, rhythm, and release.

Eco Recharge

A future-forward café vision with plants, running water, natural light, outdoor seating, and eventual EV charging.

More Than a Café. A Place to Belong.

We spend so much of life isolated behind screens, disconnected from nature, culture, and one another. Silhouette’s Café is designed to bring people back together.

For returning residents reconnecting with Jamaica after years away, the café becomes a bridge back to community, rhythm, culture, and belonging. For entrepreneurs, creatives, and professionals, it offers a space where authentic networking and meaningful business relationships can grow organically. For singles, it provides a warm and welcoming place to meet new people.

Silhouette’s Café is not just about coffee or wellness products. It is about creating a lifestyle rooted in people, nature, and authentic connection.
