Weekly Activity Menu

Building Your Emotional Wellness Muscles

WEEK 1: STRESS AND YOUR LIFE
Explore how stress shows up in your body, thoughts, and daily rhythms so you can begin responding with awareness rather than reaction.
WEEK 2: IDENTIFYING PAIN POINTS AND TRIGGERS
Gently identify emotional pain points and triggers while building compassion for your lived experiences.
WEEK 3: BODY-BASED REGULATION BASICS
Learn simple body-based practices that help calm your nervous system and restore a sense of internal safety.
WEEK 4: LETTING GO AND MOVING FORWARD
Release what no longer serves you and begin reconnecting with choice, clarity, and forward movement.
WEEK 5: THE PURSUIT OF HAPPINESS
Redefine happiness on your own terms by focusing on meaning, values, and emotional alignment.
WEEK 6: SUSTAINABLE HABITS AND DAILY PRACTICE
Create realistic habits that support emotional wellbeing long after the program ends.
7 WAYS TO REFLECT
Choose reflective practices that help you integrate learning, insight, and emotional growth.
